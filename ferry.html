New ferry service 2017

NYC'S NEW TRANSPORTATION EXPERIENCE is the NYC MTA ferry operated by Hornblower, which provides New Yorkers a dependable way to commute and connect in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx via the East River. The state-of-the-art vessels offer comfortable conveniences, including charging stations, Wi-Fi, and concessions - and they're the same price as a subway ride!
